The groups

Which group suits you?

Not sure? Come along and ask — the Club Runs Co-ordinator will point you in the right direction.

A
Group A — beginners & returners
No experience needed · Very sociable · Arranged when needed
15–20 mi
Steady pace
This is our entry-level group ride, aimed at those who haven't been on a bike for a while or who are building towards riding with Group B. It's a very friendly and sociable ride with no expectation of prior group riding experience. An experienced club member will be on hand to offer advice on gear selection, road position, group riding skills and general cycling topics.
15–20 miles No experience needed Café stop Arranged on demand
B
Group B — short & long options
Medium pace · Café stop · Re-groups on hills
25–50 mi
10.5–12 mph
A sociable medium-pace ride that includes a café stop. The short option covers around 30 miles (approx. 3 hours riding time) and the long option covers around 45 miles (approx. 4 hours). Both rides re-group regularly — particularly after hills — making them accessible to riders still building fitness. A good stepping stone toward faster groups.
25–50 miles 10.5–12 mph Café stop Re-groups on hills
C
Group C — short & long options
Faster pace · Café stop
40–70 mi
12–14 mph
For riders who want a quicker pace than Group B. A café stop is included. The short option covers 40–50 miles and the long option 50–70 miles, with the long ride typically aiming to be back by 15:00. A route is agreed by the route planner in advance, with café stop options confirmed on the day.
40–70 miles 12–14 mph Café stop Back by 15:00 (long)
D
Group D — fast & far
Through and off · Tight twos · Gravel options
70–95+ mi
13.5–15.5 mph
Social but demanding. These rides require a decent level of fitness and bike-handling ability. The group rides in tight twos and uses through-and-off technique, which creates a smoother and faster ride — this takes discipline from the whole group. Don't worry if you haven't ridden like this before; other members will help you, though if you're not comfortable with this riding style, Group D is not for you right now.

Group D rides may start earlier than other groups and include gravel rides when conditions and routes are favourable.
70–95+ miles 13.5–15.5 mph Through and off Tight twos Gravel options Early start possible
Safety

Ride guidance

All riders are expected to follow the Highway Code and ride in a manner that is safe and considerate to other road users. Club guidance documents cover road positioning, signalling, group riding etiquette and what to do if you get a puncture or mechanical.

Electric bikes

E-bike policy

Riders on electric bikes are welcome at HCC. We ask that e-bike riders join the group appropriate to their ability without motor assistance, so that the experience is consistent for the whole group. If you have questions about riding an e-bike with us, please get in touch.
