New to the club?

Your first ride

It's simpler than you think. No joining fee to try us out, no awkward introductions — just turn up and ride.

1
Pick a ride
Browse the upcoming events and find a ride that suits your level. Each listing shows the distance, pace and meeting point.
2
Message the ride leader
Use the button below to drop the ride leader a quick message so they know to expect you. That's all they need.
3
Turn up and ride
Head to the meeting point listed in the event notes. The ride leader will be looking out for you and will introduce you to the group.
4
Enjoy the ride
We ride as a group, at a pace that works for everyone. Nobody gets left behind.
5
Café stop included
Every ride includes a café stop. It's as much a part of the club as the miles — and usually the highlight.
