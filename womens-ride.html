About the ride

Why a Women's Ride?

Many of our existing ride groups are fast and demanding — and while women ride in all of them, the reality is that speed and group dynamics can make it harder for newer or less confident riders to find a way in. The Women's Ride changes that.

Cycling in West Yorkshire is tough. The hills don't care. But riding with a group of people who are there for the same reasons — to enjoy the roads, to challenge themselves, and to feel at home on a bike — makes all the difference. That's what this ride is about.

"If you don't see someone like you doing something, you're less likely to be inspired. Visibility matters."
— The thinking behind the ride

What it offers

What to expect

🚴
Confidence first
The Women's Ride is designed to be a lower-pressure environment. There's no expectation of prior group riding experience, and the pace is set by the group on the day — not dictated by a target speed.
🤝
Good company
Riding with people who are there for similar reasons makes a real difference. Expect conversation, encouragement, and a café stop. These rides are social first, athletic second.
🌄
Real roads
West Yorkshire's roads are hilly and rewarding. The Women's Ride takes on proper routes — not flat loops — with the knowledge that the group will tackle them together, at a manageable pace.
Practical details

When & where

Schedule

Ride days

The Women's Ride runs on a schedule posted to the club calendar on Ride with GPS. Check upcoming rides for exact dates — and if you're not sure when the next one is, drop us a message via the contact page.

Meeting point

Co-op car park, Holmfirth

The same start point as all other club rides. If the ride listing shows a different location, that will be clearly stated. Arrive a few minutes early if it's your first time — the ride leader will be there to meet you.

Distance

Typically 20–40 miles

Routes are chosen to be achievable for riders building fitness and confidence. You don't need to be able to complete a full Group B or C ride to enjoy the Women's Ride — that's the point.

Membership

Two free trial rides

Non-members are welcome to come along for up to two rides before joining the club. Please let us know in advance so the ride leader can expect you. Contact the Club Runs Co-ordinator to arrange this.
