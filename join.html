Membership

Join
Holmfirth CC

Friendly rides every Sunday for all abilities. Non-members are welcome to try two rides free before committing.

How it works

Three easy steps

01
Try two rides free
Come along on a Sunday morning. We ask that you contact the Club Runs Co-ordinator first so we can expect you and point you to the right group. No obligation to join.
02
Sign up on MemberMojo
Quick online sign-up takes just a few minutes. Choose your membership type and pay your annual fee. Renewals are also done here — membership is due on 1 April each year.
03
Sort your insurance
All riders need third-party liability insurance. British Cycling (Ride tier or above) or Cycling UK both qualify. First-year BC membership is available at a discount for new HCC members.

Insurance requirement

All riders taking part in club rides must hold adequate third-party liability insurance. The club strongly recommends British Cycling (Ride tier or above) or Cycling UK membership — both satisfy this requirement. If you're new to British Cycling, you can get discounted first-year membership through our club link.

If you want to enter organised races such as the White Rose Youth League, you'll need British Cycling Race Silver tier or above. It's your responsibility to ensure your insurance is adequate — the club reserves the right to verify it.
