About
the club

Holmfirth CC was formed in November 2013. We're affiliated to British Cycling, Cycling UK, CTT and the Yorkshire Cycling Federation, and are a Go Ride and Clubmark accredited club.

Look the part

Club kit

Our club kit is based on the Holmfirth colours of black, red and white and incorporates the Yorkshire Rose. We encourage all members to wear the kit with pride.

Ladies, gents and kids short-sleeve jerseys are stocked at Albion Cycles in Holmfirth.

A club shop opens periodically via Stimulus Sport — watch out for emails when it's open for orders.

Safe sport

Safeguarding

The welfare and safety of all our members, especially young riders and vulnerable adults, is our highest priority. HCC is fully committed to British Cycling's safeguarding policies.

Our Welfare Officers are Alison Hughes and Emily Murphy. Any safeguarding concerns should be reported to them directly, or to British Cycling if you prefer. All club coaches hold current DBS checks and relevant qualifications.
